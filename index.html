WELCOME TO THE ZIM LAND ENCROACHMENT WATCH

Land encroachment is the unauthorised occupation or use of land — often driven by informal settlement expansion, agricultural pressure, or illegal subdivision. We provide monitoring tools and alerts to help authorities, property owners, and conservation groups detect, document and respond to encroachment and land pollution.
